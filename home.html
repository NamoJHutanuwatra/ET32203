MY FAVORITE SONGG!!!
listen to these...

Released in 2015 as part of the Made in the A.M. album, "Perfect" is an upbeat pop anthem that talks about living in the moment and embracing an imperfect yet thrilling relationship. With catchy lyrics and a fun melody, the song captures the excitement of love without commitment, making it an ode to spontaneous adventures. It's full of confidence and charm, with a touch of rebelliousness, making it one of the standout tracks on the album.

"Payphone" by Maroon 5 featuring Wiz Khalifa is a song about the pain of a failed relationship and longing for what could have been. Adam Levine sings about standing at a payphone, trying to reach someone he loved, reflecting on how their time together was wasted and how their plans fell apart. The lyrics express regret, frustration, and the realization that theres no going back. Wiz Khalifas rap verse contrasts with the sorrowful tone, focusing on moving forward and achieving success despite the heartbreak. The song blends pop and rap elements, delivering an emotional yet catchy anthem about lost love and the struggles of letting go.

"Another Love" by Tom Odell is a melancholic song about emotional exhaustion and heartache. The narrator expresses frustration over his inability to fully love someone new because of past heartbreak. He describes feeling drained, having given his all to previous relationships, and now finding it difficult to offer the same level of love and vulnerability. The chorus, with its repeated line "I'll use another love," conveys a deep sense of longing and the hope for emotional healing.
The song is driven by a haunting piano melody, accompanied by Odell's raw, emotive vocals, which rise in intensity as he expresses his inner turmoil. "Another Love" captures the pain of being stuck in emotional limbo—wanting to love again but feeling weighed down by old scars. Its powerful lyrics and passionate delivery have made it resonate with listeners who have experienced similar feelings of emotional weariness.
